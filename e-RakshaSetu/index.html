Tamper-proof digital IDs separate identity from logs, ensuring user privacy and strong auditability.

Offline Vectored Mesh Maps

Compact downloadable maps include routes, police, hospitals, and safe spots. They work without network, critical for rescue in zero-coverage areas.

Automated Multi-Tier SOS

Silent, missing, or distress alerts are triggered by analytics plus a manual panic button.

Hotspot Monitor

Displays high-risk areas on police dashboards, enabling monitoring, while ensuring privacy-preserving access.

High Risk Zone Prediction

AI flags unsafe regions by analyzing data of Network areas, Border areas, Geo-political issues, Forest areas, Tribal areas.

AI Multi-Factor Route Safety Scoring

ML fuses data of road quality, terrain, weather, high risk areas and nearby safety hubs to recommend safest routes to the user.

Smart Logging System

Temporary logs persist only after meaningful stops, providing investigators with useful traces while minimizing data noise and retention.
