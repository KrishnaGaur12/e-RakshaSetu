About us

E-RakshaSetu is India's pioneering tourist safety ecosystem that combines AI-powered monitoring, blockchain-verified identities, and real-time emergency response to protect tourists across the nation. We're redefining travel safety with cutting-edge technology accessible even in the most remote locations.

Our Genesis

Founded with a vision to make India the safest tourist destination globally, E-RakshaSetu emerged from the critical need to address tourist safety challenges. Recognizing the gaps in traditional safety measures, we set out to create a comprehensive, technology-driven platform that ensures every traveler feels secure, from bustling cities to remote villages.

Our Vision

E-RakshaSetu aims to revolutionize tourist safety across India through innovative technology. We envision a future where every tourist—domestic or international—can explore India's incredible diversity with complete peace of mind. By integrating AI surveillance, blockchain authentication, and instant emergency response systems, we're building a safety net that works anywhere, anytime.

Our Technology

Our platform leverages state-of-the-art technologies including AI-powered threat detection, blockchain-based identity verification, end-to-end encryption for data privacy, real-time GPS tracking, and offline functionality for remote areas. The E-RakshaSetu app connects tourists with local authorities, verified guides, emergency services, and trusted accommodations—all through one seamless interface.

Our Mission

Safe Tourism, Trusted Protection, Secure India—this isn't just our tagline, it's our commitment. We're dedicated to protecting tourists while supporting local law enforcement and tourism authorities with actionable intelligence. E-RakshaSetu empowers travelers with digital safety tools, emergency SOS features, verified service provider networks, and real-time safety alerts.

Our Impact

E-RakshaSetu is transforming how India approaches tourist safety. Our platform bridges the gap between tourists and authorities, creates a safer travel ecosystem through technology, builds trust in India's tourism sector, and enables rapid emergency response even in areas with limited connectivity. Together, we're making every journey in India a safe and memorable experience.
