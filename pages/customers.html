Comprehensive Safety Features

E-RakshaSetu offers a complete suite of advanced safety features designed to protect tourists throughout their journey in India. From AI-powered monitoring to instant emergency response, discover how we're making travel safer for everyone.

AI-Powered Threat Detection: Advanced artificial intelligence continuously monitors for potential safety threats, analyzing patterns and providing real-time alerts to keep tourists informed and protected throughout their journey.

Blockchain Identity Verification: Secure, tamper-proof identity verification ensures that all service providers—guides, drivers, hotels—are authenticated and trustworthy, giving tourists confidence in every interaction.

Emergency SOS System: One-tap emergency alert instantly notifies local authorities, emergency services, and designated contacts with your exact location, even in areas with limited network connectivity.

Real-Time GPS Tracking: Share your live location with trusted contacts and authorities. Our GPS system works seamlessly to provide accurate tracking and enables rapid response in emergency situations.

Offline Functionality: Access critical safety features even without internet connection. Download area-specific safety information, emergency contacts, and maps for use in remote locations.

End-to-End Encryption: Your personal data, location history, and communications are protected with military-grade encryption, ensuring complete privacy while maintaining access for emergency services when needed.

Verified Service Network: Access a curated network of verified hotels, restaurants, tour guides, and transportation services. All partners are background-checked and continuously monitored for quality and safety standards.

Safety Alerts & Updates: Receive real-time notifications about weather conditions, local safety advisories, area-specific risks, and important travel updates to help you make informed decisions during your journey.

Multi-Language Support: Access all features in multiple languages including Hindi, English, and major regional languages, ensuring that language barriers never compromise your safety and communication needs.
